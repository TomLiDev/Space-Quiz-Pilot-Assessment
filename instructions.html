How to play

Welcome pilot. To play this quiz:

  1. Start by pressing the Begin! Button on the home page.
  2. Enter your pilot name on the page which is displayed
  3. You will then be presented with your first question, click the anser you think is correct
  4. Continue answering the questions until the quiz ends
  5. At the end of the quiz you will be presented with your final score
  6. Click the button to see where you stack up against other players on the leaderboard
